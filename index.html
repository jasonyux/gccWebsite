GCC Missions

We offer a wide-range of social, cultural, and pre-professional events. Global China Connection’s mission is to develop deep and meaningful relationships among Chinese and non-Chinese university students.

Social Events

We organize social events to facilitate the bonding of the Chinese and non-Chinese communities.

Cultural Events

We plan cultural events to celebrate out traditions and bring Chinese culture to the world.

Pre-professional Events

Jobs are important to us as well. We host pre-professional events to foster better networking.
