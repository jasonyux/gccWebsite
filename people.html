GCC Network

Global China Connection chapters materialize GCC’s goals on their own university campuses. By conducting research, leading philanthropic projects, hosting professional workshops and social events, and getting involved in global GCC initiatives, our chapters help college students around the world grow.

GCC’s Network Division offers management and individual support to chapters. This nurturing relationship has led to the spread of GCC to more than sixty campuses around the world. An added benefit to GCC’s extensive network is the opportunity for inter-chapter collaboration. GCC chapters across the world can contact each other both digitally and in person, learning from each other and pooling resources together to make otherwise impossible projects a reality.
